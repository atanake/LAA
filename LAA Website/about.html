About Us

We are a young company who understands the need for affordable and flexible accommodation in Australia.
All apartments are fully-furnished with electrical and cooking applicances/tools, beds and sheets, unlimited internet and cleaning items. Rent is inclusive of water, electricity and internet bills.
Rooms are 2 or 3-people shared, and you will get a great opportunity to make new friends with your housemates.
With minimum lease term of 1 or 2 weeks, you can book a bed in one of our many strategic, fully-furnished apartments and stay for the duration you need. You will still need to pay bond, which will be returned if there is no property damage, minus $30 cleaning/maintenance fees. Browse through our available properties, and contact us at our mobile number for inspection.
